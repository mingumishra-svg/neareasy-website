Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2025

NearEasy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

We collect your device's location to show nearby hotels. This data is used only while you are actively using the app and is not stored on our servers.

Account Information

If you choose to sign in, we collect:

  • Email address (for Google Sign-In)
  • Phone number (for Phone OTP login)
  • Display name and profile photo (if available)

Usage Data

We collect anonymous analytics about how you use the app, including:

  • Hotels you view or contact
  • Features you use
  • App crashes and errors

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To show hotels near your location
  • To enable you to contact hotels via call or WhatsApp
  • To improve our app and services
  • To provide customer support

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Google (for authentication, maps, and Places API for hotel data)
  • Firebase (for app functionality)
  • Hotel businesses (your contact details when you call/message them)

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and as required by law. You may delete your data at any time from the Settings screen in the app.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your account from the Settings screen in the app
  • Disable location access in your device settings
  • Sign out at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
